Today Lets Celebrate you

I don’t say it enough, but I’m so grateful for you

For your energy, your loyalty,

your softness, your madness.

I’ve always admired how deeply you love

And how fiercely you protect the people you care about.

It’s something rare and precious.

Your first birthday after getting married

and you’re glowing in a way

That feels so full and real.

Watching you step into this new chapter

has been such a beautiful feeling.

You’ve always carried strength, grace,

and love in you, but now it feels

like it’s radiating even more.

thanks for the friendship we made

You’ve been my person through so much.

My calm when things were messy.

My laughter on days that felt too heavy.

My reality check and biggest cheerleader.

There are very few people in this world.

who just understand without needing words,

and you’re that for me.

I wish you all the best

May your life be at ease

May all your wishes come true

This year, I wish you softness in your days,

adventure in your heart,

and a sense of peace that never leaves.

I hope you continue blooming in love,

In laughter, in everything that sets your soul on fire.

And no matter where life takes us,

I’ll always be here—cheering for you,

holding space, and loving you just the same.

You’re one of my most precious people

and I’ll never stop being thankful for you.

I love you, Shrishti
